  • About Me

    Hi!


    I'm a web developer in NYC looking for a challenge. As a recent graduate of the selective App Academy, I enjoy building projects in Ruby, Rails, and JavaScript, using libraries such as jQuery and Backbone.js.


    I enjoy the challenge of programming because of how I discovered it. I played video games for as long as I can remember, and as a teenager I started experimenting with modding tools. What started as an interest grew into a hobby and finally into a career. I think of development like beating a game: just like there is always a new level to beat or record to break, there is always a more efficient code to be written.


    Some other tidbits about me:

    • - TDD is my workflow; nothing makes my day like getting an integration suite to pass.
    • - I am most comfortable designing with MVC and REST architecture in mind. I usually start with CRUD actions and work my way up from there.
    • - Most recently I have been working with Angular and server-side Node.js, as well as C++ with the WIN32 API.
    • - I enjoy working with AI, to which I apply my psychology background, and digital audio, which I studied in college.
